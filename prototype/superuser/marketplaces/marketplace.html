Reviews & Questions

At least rating(s) required before a product rating is visible on the marketplace.
If checked, customers will see messaging that the product must be purchased in order to be reviewed.
All user generated content such as reviews, questions, comments, etc need to be approved by a Marketplace Admin before they are published.

Customer UI

User and company profile pages are where customers can enter more information about themselves. These pages are only visible within a company and are typically linked from the header.
System Admins can assign applications to users as soon as they send invitations and before users activate their accounts.

Customer Roles

Developers are able to create, edit, and publish apps in the marketplace.
System Admins can access all functionality, data, and manage all users within the company. This includes promoting or demoting other users' roles.
Enabling assisted sales exposes the Sales Support role to marketplaces, which allows an agent to make purchases on behalf of a customer.
If disabled, the CVV field will not appear as a required field during assisted sales checkout.
If enabled, marketplaces can make sales without processing payment in the billing system. Paying for products manually will assume payment was collected and will record the charges appropriately.
Enabling restricted sales support exposes the Restricted Sales Support role to marketplaces, which allows an agent to make purchases on behalf of a customer for customers they create.

Security

If enabled, the user signup form in your marketplace will include a required captcha field at the bottom.

To generate your reCAPTCHA keys, go to the Get reCAPTCHA Page, enter your marketplace domain and then copy and paste the generated keys into the fields below.

Must be 8 characters in length and contain at least one letter and one number or symbol.
Must be 8 characters in length and contain at least one letter and one number or symbol. Passwords must be changed every 90 days. The "Password Reminder" email will be sent to customers at that time if it is enabled.
Must be 10 characters in length and contain an uppercase letter, a lowercase letter, and either a number or symbol. Passwords must be changed every 90 days. The "Password Reminder" email will be sent to customers at that time if it is enabled.
When customers allow Marketplace Support Representatives (MSRs) to access their accounts, the CSR can take any action as if they were the customer. Customers must approve this access before it is granted.
